友情提示：datePicker组件取材自layui的laydate组件，在保证基础使用的基础上阉割了部分功能
常规用法
<:datePicker name="picker1" style="margin-bottom: 22px;"/> <:datePicker name="picker2" label="出生日期" inForm="true" required="true"/>
各种类型
<:datePicker name="picker3" type="year" label="年" inForm="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker4" type="month" label="年月" inForm="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker5" type="date" label="年月日" inForm="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker6" type="time" label="时分秒" inForm="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker7" type="datetime" label="datetime" inForm="true"/>
范围选择
分隔符固定为【 - 】，注意前后有空格
<:datePicker name="picker8" type="year" label="年" inForm="true" range="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker9" type="month" label="年月" inForm="true" range="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker10" type="date" label="年月日" inForm="true" range="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker11" type="time" label="时分秒" inForm="true" range="true"/> <:datePicker name="picker12" type="datetime" label="datetime" inForm="true" range="true"/>
